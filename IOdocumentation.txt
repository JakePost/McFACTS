Output file : output_mergers.dat
14 column file listing all mergers from a single run of test1.py
First row is # short parameter listing

    Parameters
    ----------
    CM : What is this?
        
    M : 
        mass of remnant?
    chi_eff : 
        chi_effective for the merger
    a_tot : 
        magnitude of spin of remnant?
    spin_angle : 
        spin angle of remnant?
    m1 :
        mass of (more massive?) progenitor
    m2 :
        mass of (less massive?) progenitor
    a1 :
        spin magnitude of m1
    a2 :
        spin magnitude of m2
    theta1 :
        spin angle of m1
    theta2 :
        spin angle of m2
    gen1 :
        generation of m1 (before merger)
    gen2 :
        generation of m2 (before merger)
    t_merge :
        time of merger after onset of AGN (assumed to appear magically at t=0)
